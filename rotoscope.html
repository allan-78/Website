Rotoscope Project: Weekly Progress

Week 1

This week focused on initial frame-by-frame tracing using Photoshop, establishing the foundational movements and character outlines, with an image background.

Week 2

Building on Week 1, this week involved refining the traced lines and adding more detail to the character's form and expressions using Photoshop, without coloring or shading.

Week 3

This week continued with tracing using Photoshop, focusing on refining lines and laying image backgrounds, without coloring or shading.

Week 4

Focus shifted to background integration using Photoshop, ensuring the rotoscoped character blended seamlessly with the chosen environment, still without coloring or shading.

Week 5

This week involved adding special effects and subtle animations using Photoshop to enhance the overall visual narrative, maintaining the tracing and background focus.

Week 6

Refinement of timing and pacing was key this week using Photoshop, ensuring the animation flowed naturally and effectively conveyed motion, without coloring or shading.

Week 7

This week marked a shift to a more creative approach, introducing words, coloring, and tracing to add an auditory layer and visual depth to the storytelling.

Week 8

Continuing the creative approach, this week focused on optimizing the animation for smooth playback, incorporating words, coloring, and tracing for more artistic freedom.

Week 9

With a creative mindset, review and feedback incorporation were central this week, making final adjustments based on critiques to further enhance the artistic vision, including words, coloring, and tracing.

Week 10

Embracing creativity, polishing touches were applied, including minor color corrections and detail enhancements to elevate the animation's quality and artistic expression, with continued use of words, coloring, and tracing.

Week 11

In this creative phase, final checks for consistency and continuity across all frames were performed, ensuring a cohesive and artistically unified visual experience, integrating words, coloring, and tracing.

Week 12

The last week involved rendering the complete creatively enhanced animation and preparing it for final presentation, showcasing the artistic journey with words, coloring, and tracing.
